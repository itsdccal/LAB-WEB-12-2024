History of the Porsche

Beginning of the Legendary Sports Car

Porsche was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Germany. Porsche is known as a manufacturer of elegant and high-performance sports cars. The iconic Porsche 911 model was first launched in 1964, and it has since become a symbol of perfection in automotive design and technology.

Logo Porsche

The Porsche logo is one of the most recognizable symbols in the automotive industry. It is a representation of strength, history, and the company's connection to its homeland. The logo combines elements of the Coat of Arms of Stuttgart (a prancing horse) and the Württemberg state crest, representing Porsche's roots in the region.
